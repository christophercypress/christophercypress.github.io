About Me!

My name is Christopher Cypress. I am a self taught artist that realized far too late that I had the power to academically challenge and nurture my skills without the need of putting myself through the wonderful world of student debt. I am currently taking on the challenge of developing a hand-drawn style video game with a small team of programmers in the hopes to further push myself towards my dream of using my talent to share animation with the world. I hope you enjoy the thoughts of this single artist!
